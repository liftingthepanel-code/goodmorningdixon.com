From the Editor

Written by a neighbor,
for neighbors.

I started Good Morning Dixon because I love this town and wanted one place to get everything local — without hunting through five different websites or endless Facebook groups.

I'm a Dixon resident, not a media company. I care about this community, and I want every Dixon resident to feel a little more connected to the place we call home.
